Geosynthetic Systems

Coastal & Erosion Protection

Our coastal and erosion protection systems are designed to safeguard shorelines, riverbanks, and critical infrastructure from hydraulic forces and environmental degradation. These solutions provide robust, long-term protection against erosion, scouring, and wave impact.

Concrete Mattresses

Articulated concrete block mattresses (ACBM) for flexible, durable bank and channel protection. Includes hollow-core Terracrete blocks that promote vegetation establishment.

Enquire

Grout-Filled Bags

Fabric bags pumped with grout on-site to form a rigid, interlocking revetment. Available in 20kg, 25kg and 70kg configurations for varied application depths and exposures.

Enquire

Revetment / ACBM Systems

Engineered revetment systems combining geotextile underlayers with ACBM or concrete block facing for comprehensive slope and bank stabilisation against wave action and seepage.

Ground Stabilisation Systems

Our ground stabilisation systems are developed to improve weak or unstable soils, enabling them to safely support structural and operational loads. These solutions enhance bearing capacity, reduce settlement, and ensure reliable performance in demanding environments.

Interlocking Concrete Systems

Modular interlocking concrete units for retaining walls, slope stabilisation, and load-bearing platforms. Includes Terraforce hollow-core blocks that promote vegetation and drainage.

Enquire

Reinforced Paving Solutions

Geocell and geogrid-reinforced paving layers for access roads, car parks, and working platforms over poor ground. Reduces material costs and extends pavement life.

Enquire

Load Support Platforms

Engineered ground platforms designed to distribute heavy equipment loads over soft or variable soils. Critical for crane pads, temporary works, and industrial sites.
