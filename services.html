Three core disciplines. One integrated team. We design, specify, supply and build, so the outcome is never someone else's problem.

01. Geosynthetics

Geosynthetics Engineering

Geosynthetic materials are the hidden backbone of modern infrastructure. We design and install reinforcement systems, filtration layers, and drainage composites that extend asset life and reduce long-term maintenance costs.

  • Ground Reinforcement

    Geogrid and geotextile systems for roads, embankments, and retaining structures.

  • Drainage & Filtration

    Geocomposite drainage systems that manage subsurface water without clogging.

  • Erosion Control

    Erosion control geomats, turf reinforcement mats, and slope stabilization systems.

  • Containment Liners

    HDPE geomembrane lining systems for ponds, lagoons, and waste containment.

Start a Geosynthetics Project
02. Marine

Marine & Coastal Protection

Nigeria's coastline faces continuous pressure from erosion, storm surge, and sea-level change. We engineer coastal defence systems that work with natural processes rather than against them.

  • Shoreline Stabilization

    Revetments, seawalls, and rock armour systems engineered for site-specific wave conditions.

  • Breakwater Construction

    Rubble mound and concrete armour breakwaters designed for ports, harbours, and beaches.

  • Scour Protection

    Bridge pier and pipeline scour protection using rock, mattresses, and geosynthetic systems.

  • Coastal Flood Defence

    Flood embankments and surge barriers protecting low-lying communities and infrastructure.

Discuss a Coastal Project
03. Civil

Civil Infrastructure

Roads, drainage, and ground improvement form the foundation of urban and industrial growth. We bring the same precision to civil works that we bring to specialist geotechnical projects.

  • Road & Highway Construction

    Subbase design, pavement construction, and road rehabilitation with geosynthetic enhancement.

  • Drainage Systems

    Surface and subsurface drainage networks for roads, estates, and industrial facilities.

  • Ground Improvement

    Soft ground treatment, vibro-compaction, and dynamic compaction for challenging sites.

  • Earthworks & Embankments

    Cut and fill earthworks, reinforced embankments, and slope stabilization.

Our Supply Range

Products we supply & install

Sourced directly from our OEM partners — Techfab India, Inland Tarp & Liner, and Terraforce — every product we specify is one we install ourselves.

Geotextiles

Woven and non-woven geotextiles for separation, filtration, drainage, and reinforcement applications. Available in a range of strengths and hydraulic conductivities to suit every project condition.

Geocells

3D cellular confinement systems for road subbase reinforcement, slope protection, and channel lining. Dramatically reduce fill material requirements while increasing load-bearing performance.

Geotubes & Geobags

High-strength geotextile tubes and bags for shoreline stabilisation, dewatering, and hydraulic containment. Ideal for coastal and riverbank protection where traditional hard armour is impractical.

Geomembranes

HDPE and LLDPE geomembrane liners for ponds, lagoons, landfill cells, and mining containment. Precision-fabricated by Inland Tarp & Liner for demanding containment requirements.

TechMatt Concrete Mattress

Articulated concrete block mattresses for river scour protection, coastal revetments, and pipeline cover. Flexible enough to follow seabed contours while providing durable armour protection.

Pavers & Retaining Wall Blocks

Terraforce hollow-core interlocking concrete blocks for vegetated retaining walls and slope stabilisation. 45+ years of global proven performance with an ecological finish that promotes natural growth.
