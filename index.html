Recent Work

Projects on the ground

Every project is a commitment kept. Here are a few recent ones.

Civil

Bridge Pier Abutment Work for DESOPADEC/DELTA STATE MOW, Uzere (Isoko), Delta State

Pier and abutment construction for the Delta State Oil Producing Areas Development Commission at Uzere, Isoko South LGA. Scope included rebar placement, formwork, and concrete works to restore full load-bearing capacity to the crossing.

Completed 2024
Civil

ACBM Manufacture and Installation for NEWMAP, Edo State

Manufacture and installation of Terracrete articulated concrete block mattresses (ACBM) under the Nigeria Erosion and Watershed Management Project (NEWMAP), Edo State. Hollow-core blocks protect channel banks from erosion while allowing natural vegetation to establish through the openings.

Completed 2022/2023
Geosynthetics

Installation of Geocell at Agbede, Edo State for NGC

Supply, laying, and mechanical filling of geocell panels for ground reinforcement and slope stabilisation at Agbede, Edo State, for NGC. Geocell sections were expanded, anchored, and filled using a CAT bulldozer.

Completed 2023
